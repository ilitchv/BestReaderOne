How to play

  1. Pick three numbers from 0 to 9 or opt for Quick Pick.
  2. Decide on a $0.50 or $1 wager.
  3. Choose a play type: Straight, Box, Straight/Box, Front Pair, Back Pair, or Combination.
  4. Pick the draw time: midday, evening, or both.
  5. Choose if you want to participate in multiple consecutive draws.
  6. Check the results to see if you've won.
Ticket cost
$0.50 or $1 per play.
$1 for the Straight/Box play type.
$1.50, $3, or $6 for the Combination play type.

Play types

Play Type Description
Straight Match the winning numbers in the exact order.
Box Match the winning numbers in any order.
  • 3-Way Box - two numbers are identical.
  • 6-Way Box - all three numbers are different.
Front Pair Match the first two numbers.
Back Pair Match the last two numbers.
Straight/Box Match the winning numbers in either exact or any order (combines a Straight and a Box play type). Win both prizes for an exact match.
  • 3-Way Straight/Box - two numbers are identical.
  • 6-Way Straight/Box - all three numbers are different.
Combination Match the winning numbers in any order - all combinations are considered straight plays.
  • 3-Way Combination - two numbers are identical.
  • 6-Way Combination - all three numbers are different.

Things to know

  1. There are six play types.
  2. Hand your ticket over to a retailer within 30 days of purchase to play the same numbers again for the next draw using the Play It Again feature.
  3. Increase your winnings by 5%, 10%, or 20% when the Booster Ball promotion is active, at no additional cost.
  4. Play the same numbers for up to seven consecutive draws.
