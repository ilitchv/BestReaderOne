Lottery Game Rules

Pick 3 & Pick 4 Games

Most state lotteries offer daily Pick 3 and Pick 4 games with multiple draws throughout the day. Here's how they work:

Pick 3 Rules:

  • Choose 3 numbers from 0 to 9
  • Select your play type: Straight, Box, Straight/Box, or Combination
  • Choose your wager amount (typically $0.50 or $1.00 per play)
  • Select which draw(s) to play (morning, day, evening, night)
  • Prizes vary by state and play type

Pick 4 Rules:

  • Choose 4 numbers from 0 to 9
  • Select your play type: Straight, Box, Straight/Box, or Combination
  • Choose your wager amount (typically $0.50 or $1.00 per play)
  • Select which draw(s) to play (midday, evening, night)
  • Prizes vary by state and play type

Prize Information

Straight Play

Win by matching the numbers in the exact order drawn. Example: You play 123 and the winning number is 123.

Typical prize: $500 per $1 wagered

Box Play

Win by matching all the numbers in any order. Example: You play 123 and the winning number is 321.

Typical prize: $80 per $1 wagered (for 3-way box)

Straight/Box Combo

Combines both Straight and Box play types for more chances to win.

Typical prize: Varies based on wager split

Combination Play

Covers all possible Straight combinations of your selected numbers.

Typical prize: $500 per $1 wagered (divided by number of combinations)
